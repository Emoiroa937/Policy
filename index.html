Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Thank you for using the Auto Workflow Integrator extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy and data. This policy explains how our extension operates and handles data.

1. Data Collection and Use

This extension DOES NOT collect, store, sell, or share any personal user data with third parties. The extension operates entirely locally on your browser for the sole purpose of automating repetitive administrative tasks.

2. Requested Permissions

  • Host Permissions: The extension only operates on specifically designated internal document management domains (qlvb.tphcm.gov.vn, dhtn.dcs.vn, phuongbinhtay.vn). It interacts with the web interface (DOM) to simulate user clicks and form filling.
  • Storage: We use chrome.storage.local to temporarily store operational commands (e.g., document IDs, action states) to maintain the automation workflow when a page reloads. This data is stored locally on your device and is automatically cleared once the process is complete.

3. Authentication Information

The extension does not read, record, or interfere with passwords, PINs, or any security authentication information of the user.
